PigEye - CASE STUDY

Elevating Livestock Management Through Smart Farming

In an industry where precision and real-time decision-making can directly impact animal welfare and farm profitability, PigEye emerges as a forward-thinking solution. Designed specifically for pig farms, this intelligent platform leverages AI, IoT, and data-driven insights to digitize and streamline every aspect of livestock operations.

Client Overview

PigEye is a modern web-based livestock management platform designed specifically for pig farms. It provides farm owners with a centralized, digital solution for tracking, maintaining, and analyzing every aspect of pig farm operations. The system aims to bring digital transformation to traditional farming environments by offering a smart interface, powerful data tracking, and integrated AI monitoring, all accessible via the cloud. With support for multiple languages (English and Chinese), PigEye caters to a diverse user base across different regions.

The Challenge

Managing a pig farm is an inherently complex task, involving dynamic biological systems, changing environmental factors, and constant operational oversight. In traditional settings, this complexity was compounded by outdated methods of recordkeeping and monitoring. Farm owners were often forced to rely on manual headcounts, paper-based logs, and human observation to track pig behavior, health, and farm productivity.

However, the absence of automation and centralization led to significant challenges:

High Risk of Human Error

Manual headcounting and behavior monitoring of pigs are not only time-consuming but often inaccurate, especially on large-scale farms.

Lack of Real-Time Visibility

There was no system in place to provide live insight into pig movement or detect anomalies in real time.

Unstructured Historical Data

Previous farm data was often scattered across spreadsheets, paper files, or personal notes, making it nearly impossible to track long-term trends or analyze patterns.

Missed Anomalies

Without intelligent video analysis, subtle changes in animal behavior—early signs of illness or injury—went unnoticed.

Environmental Data Gaps

Critical farm environmental factors such as humidity, temperature, ammonia levels, and energy consumption were not being tracked or visualized in real-time.

Lack of Predictive Insights

Farms had no ability to leverage historical and live data to forecast production issues, health events, or environmental risks.

Language and Accessibility Barriers

Many software tools lacked proper multilingual interfaces or were too technical for users without IT backgrounds.

The problem was not simply about building a digital farm tool—it was about solving a deeply rooted, multidisciplinary issue: integrating AI, IoT, and real-time analytics into agriculture in a way that’s user-friendly, reliable, and valuable at scale.

Solution Approach

PigEye delivers a holistic and intelligent solution designed to meet every operational challenge faced by modern pig farms. Its architecture is a synergistic blend of AI, IoT integration, data visualization, and user-centered design—all working together to improve livestock management, environmental monitoring, and performance tracking.

YOLO (You Only Look Once)

Real-time object detection from farm cameras to identify and count pigs with high precision, handling varied lighting, occlusions, and crowded pens.

Deep SORT

Continuously tracks each pig across video frames to avoid double-counting, monitor movement, and analyze time spent in specific areas.

Mask R-CNN

Performs instance segmentation to isolate individual pigs at a pixel level. The segmented area is then used to estimate pig weight through calibrated pixel-to-weight models, enabling non-invasive growth tracking.

Custom Heatmap Engine

Aggregates movement data to generate heatmaps of pig activity, highlighting hotspots and unusual behavior patterns that can signal stress or illness.

Hardware Sensor Integration and IoT Data Streaming

PigEye supports real-time data ingestion from on-farm hardware sensors measuring temperature, humidity, CO2 levels, ammonia concentration, and energy usage.

These sensor inputs are visualized in charts and dashboards, and used to trigger smart alerts when thresholds are crossed.

The system ensures live synchronization of sensor data across devices, enabling immediate corrective actions.

Smart Data Visualization and UX

All critical parameters are translated into digestible charts, graphs, and summary cards.

Users can toggle between daily, weekly, or monthly views and filter results based on barn, pig type, or time period.

The platform includes detailed visualizations for pig health, feeding patterns, growth trajectory, breeding cycles, financial summaries, and environment analytics.

Modular Web Architecture with Interactive Features

Each tab—Dashboard, Health Monitoring, Growth Analytics, Breeding, Financials, and more—features purpose-built charts like dual-line temperature graphs, vaccination heatmaps, pie-based cost breakdowns, and ADG (Average Daily Gain) charts.

Users can configure alerts, generate PDF/Excel reports, and set custom thresholds.

Manual data entry options are available for flexibility, ensuring usability even without live feeds or sensors.

Data Security & Accessibility

Cloud-based architecture allows users to access the platform from any location.

Multilingual interface ensures inclusivity for both English- and Chinese- speaking teams.

Secure authentication and role-based access protect sensitive farm data.

The Outcome

PigEye has transformed traditional pig farming into a future-ready, data-driven operation. By leveraging cutting-edge AI, real-time sensor data, and modular analytics, PigEye empowers farmers to take smarter actions, earlier.

Precision Livestock Monitoring

Real-time pig detection, tracking, and weight estimation ensures accurate and ongoing animal insight.

Environmental Intelligence

Seamless IoT integration allows farms to proactively manage temperature, humidity, gas levels, and energy use.

Operational Efficiency

From breeding cycles to feed conversion ratios, PigEye automates and visualizes every metric that matters.

Anomaly Detection & Alerts

Heatmaps and alert logic identify behavioral and environmental risks before they escalate.

Scalable Architecture

Easily accommodates farms of all sizes, with modular design to expand functionality as needed.

Comprehensive Oversight

Interactive charts and dashboards offer end-to- end visibility—livestock, health, environment, finances—in one system.

Predictive Capabilities

Historical patterns combined with live data enable forecasting of production outcomes and health risks.

Global Adaptability

Multi-language, cloud-based access ensures PigEye fits seamlessly into diverse agricultural contexts.

PigEye proves that smart farming is not only possible but practical—delivering measurable value through automation, analytics, and intelligent design.
