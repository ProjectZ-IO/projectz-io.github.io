How Figma Solves Design Challenges

Designers often face fragmented workflows, version conflicts, and limited collaboration tools. Figma provides solutions by:

  • Enabling real-time multi-user collaboration on the same design file.
  • Centralizing design components and assets for consistent branding.
  • Allowing interactive prototyping without additional software.
  • Reducing iteration time through cloud-based feedback and version control.
